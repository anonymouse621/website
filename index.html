So, you decided you wanted to sail the high seas? Great! Here's a guide with everything you need to know.

Browser

First, you'll need a browser. I recommend librewolf. It's an open source privacy focused browser based off firefox, and is perfect for our task.

After you've installed librewolf, click on the uBlock origin extension icon (next to the browser menu) and open the dashboard. (If uBlock Origin isn't installed on your browser, search 'uBlock origin' on your prefered extension store.)

While in the dashboard, head over to the filters list tab and tick these boxes. Under that, you'll find the 'Regions, languages' section. select 'ru ua uz kz: RU AdList' and your own lamgauge there.
